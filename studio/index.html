System Q

Session-ready studio architecture: central control surface, left/right processing racks, modular cube I/O, and software that keeps one mental model from preamp to monitors. Part of the same proof-of-concept ecosystem as Accountable Infinite Prosperity—culture and evidence, not escape pods.

WHAT THIS IS

A unified recording and monitoring environment: operator surface, rack DSP/analog stages, floor/desktop “cube” nodes, and networked musician Rigs—designed so artists plug in, route, and track without a pile of incompatible interfaces.
