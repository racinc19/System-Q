GIG Racks

Touchscreen racks with swappable analog cards.

Racks are the premium GIG path: two touchscreen-faced analog frames with modular PCB cards for preamp, compressor, EQ, tone, routing, monitoring, summing, and recall.

Touchscreen Face

The rack front is the display and touch surface for channel focus, metering, and signal-stage control.

Swappable Cards

Instead of replacing the whole box, slide in different analog PCB cards for preamps, compressors, EQ, and tone modules.

Analog Character

Clean, colored, tube, harmonic, compression, EQ, saturation, and output behavior.

Hybrid Recall

Digital control remembers analog and hybrid signal-chain decisions.

Two touchscreen faces: left rack for channel processing, right rack for monitoring, summing, output, and Venue handoff.
Card-frame concept: analog cards slide into internal slots, more like a modular computer backplane than fixed outboard gear.
Swappable preamps: clean, transformer, tube, colored, or future specialty front-end cards.
Swappable dynamics/EQ: compressor, limiter, gate, EQ, transient, saturation, and exciter modules can evolve by card family.
Digital control: the touchscreen and Control surface steer the analog card stack with recall.
