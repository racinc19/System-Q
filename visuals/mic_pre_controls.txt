Mic pre cell — three hardware-style controls (concept)
-------------------------------------------------------
1. 48V     — phantom on/off (knob + LED state)
2. HPF     — high-pass: knob selects corner frequency (e.g. Off / 40 / 80 / 120 Hz — set in product spec)
3. Phase   — polarity invert (often labeled Ø or PHASE; 180° — not 90° unless you add that later)
